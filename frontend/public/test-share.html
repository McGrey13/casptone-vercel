⚙️ Technical Details

This page includes the following Open Graph meta tags:

  • og:title - The title shown in the preview
  • og:description - The description text
  • og:image - The preview image
  • og:url - The canonical URL
  • og:type - The type of content (website)
  • og:site_name - Your site name (CraftConnect)
  • twitter:card - Twitter card type (summary_large_image)
